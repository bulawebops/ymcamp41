Camp Information

We are excited to announce the Lehi Utah 3rd Stake Young Men Camp! This is a fantastic opportunity for all the Young Men to grow together in faith, develop character, and make lifelong memories in the great outdoors.

  • 📍 Camp Location: Currently Pending (1-2 hours out from Lehi)
  • 🗓️ Camp Date: June 29 - July 2, 2026
  • 👤 Who Attends: All Young Men

💵 Camp Fees

$10.00 per Young Man

The camp fee covers meals, camp site fees, and a camp t-shirt. Please fill out a tithing slip, under the Other option - write "YM Stake Camp". This will help the Clerks to transfer these fees to the Stake.

⚠️ Deadline: Sunday, April 12 — all payments must be submitted by this date.

📝 Camp Registration

Use this form to register youth who are committing to attend camp and pay the $10.00 fee.

Questions

Please contact your Ward Young Men Camp Directors Brother Bowman and Brother Faga for more information.
