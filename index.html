Camp Information

We are excited to announce the Lehi Utah 3rd Stake Young Men Camp! This is a fantastic opportunity for young men to grow together in faith, develop character, and make lifelong memories in the great outdoors.

  • 📍 Location: To be announced — watch for updates from your ward leaders
  • 🗓️ Date: Details provided by your ward Young Men presidency
  • 👤 Who: All young men in the Lehi Utah 3rd Stake

💵 Camp Fee

$10 per young man

The camp fee covers meals, activities, and a camp t-shirt. Please submit payment to your ward Young Men leader or bishopric representative.

⚠️ Deadline: Sunday, April 12 — all payments must be in by this date.

👕 T-Shirt Size

Every young man who registers will receive a camp t-shirt. Please provide your preferred size when you submit payment.

✅ How to Register

  1. Choose your t-shirt size using the chart above.
  2. Prepare $10 cash or check (checks payable to your ward).
  3. Submit payment and your t-shirt size to your ward Young Men leader or bishopric representative.
  4. Deadline: Sunday, April 12 — late registrations may not receive a t-shirt in their requested size.

❓ Questions?

Please contact your ward's Young Men president or bishopric for more information. You can also reach out to your Stake Young Men presidency.
